Alsace

Domaine Burckel-Jung

Domaine Burckel-Jung is located in Gertwiller, France, a small picturesque town at the foot of Mount Saint Odile. This winery has been passed down through eight generations and is still operated by the same family, who recently decided to take the production 100% organic.

Languedoc

Clos Centeilles

Clos Centeilles is located in Centeilles, a small town in the Minervois subregion of Languedoc. The estate is run by Patricia Boyer Domergue who studied at the Institute of Enology in Bordeaux. In 1995 she became devoted to replanting forgotten ancient vines, providing this winery an incredible uniqueness.

Wines

  • Crèmant d'Alsace
  • Crèmant d'Alsace Rosé
  • Riesling
  • Gewurztraminer
  • Pinot Noir

Muscadet sèvre et maine

Domaine Girard

Domaine Girard is a family estate, Patrick Girard has been the owner since 1991 and in 2013 his son, Guillaume, joined him. The estate is located between the rivers Sèvre & Maine, in the heart of Muscadet. The main production is the Muscadet Sèvre et Maine sur lie (90% of the vineyard surface area). The vineyard is planted on schist and gneiss subsoils which gives elegant fruity aromas and very interesting mineral hints to the wine. The wine is left on its lees for 8 to 15 months to increase its richness.
